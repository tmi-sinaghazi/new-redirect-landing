Explore the SMLC Model

The SMLC model is designed to be interactive and adaptable, allowing leaders to explore different scenarios and understand the multifaceted nature of leadership.

Dimensions of the Cube

1. Personal Dimension

Investigate how personal values, beliefs, and behaviors influence leadership styles. This dimension encourages self-reflection and personal development.

2. Organizational Dimension

Understand the role of organizational culture, structure, and processes. Explore how these factors affect decision-making, communication, and overall effectiveness.

3. Societal Dimension

Examine the broader societal context, including economic systems, cultural norms, and technological advancements. Learn how external factors shape organizational and personal leadership approaches.

Interactive Tools

Utilize our interactive cube and sliders to model different leadership scenarios. Adjust variables to see how changes at one level impact others.

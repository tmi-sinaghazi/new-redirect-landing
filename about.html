About SMLC

About Sina's Multidimensional Leadership Cube

The Multidimensional Leadership Cube (SMLC) is a comprehensive model developed by Sina to explore the complexities of leadership across different dimensions and levels. It integrates various factors that influence leadership styles and effectiveness, providing a framework for leaders to assess and develop their capabilities.

Background and Inspiration

Recognizing that leadership is not one-dimensional, the SMLC model was inspired by the need to understand how personal traits, organizational structures, and societal contexts interact to shape leadership dynamics. It draws upon interdisciplinary research and practical experiences to offer a holistic view.

Key Components

  • Personal Dimension: Focuses on individual traits, values, and behaviors.
  • Organizational Dimension: Examines the impact of organizational culture, structure, and processes.
  • Societal Dimension: Considers societal norms, economic systems, and cultural influences.

Mission and Vision

The mission of the SMLC model is to empower leaders with the tools and insights needed to navigate the complexities of today's world effectively. The vision is to foster a generation of leaders who are adaptable, empathetic, and strategically minded.
